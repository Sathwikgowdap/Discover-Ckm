Shri Deviramma Temple

Shri Deviramma Temple is an ancient shrine nestled atop the Deviramma Hill in the Chandra Drona hills near Chikmagalur, Karnataka, known for its spiritual significance and breathtaking views. The temple is dedicated to Goddess Deviramma, a peaceful manifestation of Goddess Durga. It is unique as it opens only once a year on Naraka Chaturdashi, the day before Diwali, attracting lakhs of devotees for the annual darshan.
